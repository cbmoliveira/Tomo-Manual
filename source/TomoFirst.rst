Study Permit
------------

The Study Permit is almost the most important 
document during your studies in Canada, it legitimizes 
your stay in this country. Don't confuse it with your student 
visa, which is affixed to your passport and lets you into Canada. The Study Permit is an A4-sized document you obtain when entering the country.
